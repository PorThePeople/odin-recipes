Namtok

Ingredients

  • Pork neck or belly
  • Puffed rice
  • Shallots
  • Mint leaves
  • Fish sauce
  • Lime
  • Thai chili powder
  • Optional: Dried chilis
  • Optional: Cucumber or salad greens

Steps

  1. Slice the pork into thin strips and marinate if preferred
  2. Grill the pork strips until 75% cooked, wrap in aluminium
  3. Chop the shallots, pick the mint leaves, and crush the puffed rice
  4. Mix lime, fish sauce, and the chili powder
  5. Add the cooked pork into mixture and toss until fully covered
  6. Add the puffed rice and toss until well mixed
  7. Add the shallots and mint leaves
  8. Serve and enjoy! Recommended pairings: fresh cucumber, salad greens, or sticky rice!
