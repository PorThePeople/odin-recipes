Somtum

What is Somtum? Somtum, green papaya spicy salad, is a common dish found in South East Asia countries

Ingredients

  • Green papaya
  • Carrots
  • Fish sauce
  • Dried shrimps
  • Coconut or palm sugar
  • Limes
  • Chillis of your choice, de-stemmed

Steps

  1. Slice the green papaya and carrots into thin strips and qucikly rinse under cold water
  2. Add sugar, fish sauce, and lime juice into the mortar, pound until the sugar is dissolved
  3. Add the chillis and dried shrimps, gently pound to release flavour
  4. Add the prepped carrots and papaya, toss to coat
  5. Plate, and dress with dried shrimps
  6. Serve and enjoy!
