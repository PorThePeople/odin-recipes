Thai Tea Recipe!

Sorry, no pictures here!

Ingredients

  • Thai tea leave (assam tea)
  • Condensed milk
  • Evaporated milk
  • Ice

Steps

  1. Boil the tea leaves in water
  2. Remove the tea leaves and keep the pot covered
  3. In a cup, add the warm thai tea and condensed milk
  4. Stir well until mixed
  5. Add evaporated milk until desired consistency
  6. Pour into a cup with ice
  7. Drizzle more evaporated milk
  8. Enjoy!
